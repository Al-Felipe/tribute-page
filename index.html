About

Pantera was a heavy metal band from Arlington, Texas, formed in 1981, by the brothers Vinnie Paul and Dimebag Darrell. Their fifth album released in 1990 called "Cowboys from Hell" popularized the groove metal genre. Pantera is regarded as one of the most successful and influential bands in heavy metal history
